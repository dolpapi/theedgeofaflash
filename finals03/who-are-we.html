“The Edge of a Flash” is an advocacy campaign plan with the goal of ending fast fashion. The title implies a hopeful but quick resolution to the issue at hand. We want to promote conscious consumerism, especially in fashion. We believe we can create a fashion industry that respects both people and the planet, ensuring a brighter future for all. We recognize that excessive water usage and toxic chemical releases within fast fashion wreak havoc on ecosystems and contribute significantly to climate change.

Our mission is to prioritize sustainability in fashion, challenging fast fashion norms, advocating for conscious consumption and production. We inspire individuals and businesses to build a more just, sustainable, and socially conscious fashion ecosystem.
